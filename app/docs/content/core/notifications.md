# Instructions
## Viewing Notifications
You can view your notifications from two places in the application.

1. Click on the bell icon in the top menu bar
2. Navigate to the [notifications page]({{ url_for("core.all_notifications") }}) from the notification dropdown or notifications settings page

From the dropdown menu, you will be shown all unread notifications from the last five days. You can scroll through this list
and click the checkmark beside a notification to mark it as read. If a notification has a link associated with it, you can click on the "view" link to follow it.

From the notifications page, you will be shown all of your notifications. You can search through these with the text field at the top of the page.
From here, you can mark a notification as read by clicking the "mark read" button next to it.
If a notification has a link associated with it, you can click on the "view" button to follow it.
Notifitions that have already been read will be grayed out.

_Note: You may also receive notifications via email or text depending on your settings._
